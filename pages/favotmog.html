Characters


Arthas, The Lich King

Arthas Menethil, initially a paladin of the Silver Hand and the crown prince of Lordaeron, Arthas was corrupted by the cursed blade Frostmourne in an attempt to save his people, leading him to become the Lich King.

Anduin Wrynn

Anduin Wrynn is the son of Varian Wrynn, the former King of Stormwind, and the current leader of the Alliance, succeeding his father after his death. Anduin is known for his compassionate and peaceful nature, often trying to find diplomatic solutions rather than resorting to violence.

Varian Wrynn

Varian Wrynn was the King of Stormwind and a prominent figure in the Alliance. Varian's character is known for his fierce vendetta against the orcs and the Horde, stemming from witnessing the orcs' invasion of Azeroth during the First War. Varian's character development includes leading campaigns for unity among the Alliance races and facing pivotal moments such as sacrificing himself to save his faction from the Legion.

Uther the Lighbringer

Uther the Lightbringer is a paladin known for his dedication to the Holy Light and his role as a mentor, instructor, and friend to Prince Arthas Menethil. Uther played a crucial part in the Knights of the Silver Hand, a paladin order he helped found, and was recognized for his heroism during the Second War of Azeroth. His story involves training Arthas, aiding in battles against the undead Scourge, and ultimately facing challenges that led to his tragic demise. Uther is remembered for his unwavering commitment to defending his people and his nation, embodying noble qualities despite his imperfections.

Illidan Stormrage

Illidan Stormrage is a former night elf sorcerer and the first of the demon hunters, known for his pursuit of power and his role as the former self-proclaimed Lord of Outland and ruler of the Black Temple. Illidan made a pact with the Burning Legion to secure the entrance of its leader, Sargeras, into Azeroth, which earned him the nickname "the Betrayer." He eventually returned to the side of his fellow night elves, fighting for the defense of Azeroth. Illidan is currently the jailer of Sargeras, guarding his prison far away from Azeroth.
